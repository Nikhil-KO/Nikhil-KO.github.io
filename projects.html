Current Projects



QueueTube

Created for my cumpulsion to watch ALL the videos in my subscriptions in the order they were released. This web app is being developed to automatically create and maintain a database of videos to watch. It uses Google OAuth2 to securely access your subscribed channels, using this information and the YouTube API, a database of videos to watch is built for each user. The app will be hosted on Heroku Free Tier once it is completed.
